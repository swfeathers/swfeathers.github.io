Sam Featherstone

About

I'm Sam, people call me Feathers.

I'm based Battersea in South London and I produce music. Rock and pop is what I love the most, but I get excited by all good music no matter the genre.

My work in the music world started when I played guitar in UK rock band Me and the Mountain. In the band I recorded, produced and mixed all the music as well as playing guitar on the tracks (I even drummed on our first EP!), but don't let me near a vocal mic — no-one wants to hear that.

I loved being in the studio crafting recordings so I gradually migrated over to that world. Having managed the recording studio at Price Studios for 2½ years, I'm now back freelancing and looking to set up a studio of my own.

If you're a band or artist and are interested in working with me then get in touch, I'd love to hear from you.
