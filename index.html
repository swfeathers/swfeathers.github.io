Sam Featherstone

Music Producer / Audio Engineer

About

Based in Battersea in South London, Sam Featherstone is an engineer, producer and songwriter. Whilst his forte is in Rock and Pop music, his credits stretch across many genres.

Sam's music career started playing guitar in UK rock band Me and the Mountain, before migrating over to the studio world.

Having managed the recording studio at Price Studios for 2½ years, Sam is now back freelancing and looking to set up a studio of his own.
