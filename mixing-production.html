Sam Featherstone

Music Producer / Mix Engineer

Mixing

Send me your tracks and I'll make them sound better.

I've worked on all types of genres, so whether it's Metal or Folk, Jazz or Classical I will be able to bring a professional sheen to your work.

I'll ask you about who your favourite artists are and what you want your music to sound like, that way we'll be on the same page and I can quickly get the sound your looking for.

All I ask is that the material has been recorded well. For that reason I will usually ask that you send me some material ahead of time, so that I can check everything over. At that stage I will also be able to quote an appropriate price.

If the tracks still need a bit of editing or tuning etc. then we can talk about that too. Furthermore if there are some production elements you'd still like to add, we can look into that. More about that below.

Production

Got a song demo and need help taking it to the next level?

Then I'm your man. I love the challenge of helping someone realise their song's potential.

I grew up playing in bands, so writing songs and working on arrangements is something I love doing. I could talk about the different production elements I like to bring to a project, but it might be easier to just have a listen.

Play one of tracks on the right to see what I added to the original demos. Make sure to listen to the different sections whilst switching between 'Demo' and 'Master' to hear what I added.

Whether it's drums, guitars, synths, strings or dirty bass, I can help you bring your demos to life.
