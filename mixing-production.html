Sam Featherstone

Music Producer / Mix Engineer

Mixing

Send me your tracks and I'll make them sound better.

I've worked on all types of genres, so whether it's Metal or Folk, Jazz or Classical I will be able to bring a professional sheen to your work.

I'll ask you about who your favourite artists are and what you want your music to sound like, that way we'll be on the same page and I can quickly get the sound your looking for.

All I ask is that the material has been recorded well. For that reason I will usually ask that you send me one of the tracks ahead of time, so that I can check everything over. At that stage I will also be able to quote an appropriate price. I.e. mixing a vocal to a prepared backing track is not going to cost as much as an monster session with multitracked drums, an orchestra and three choirs' worth of backing vocals!

If the tracks still need a bit of editing or tuning etc. then we can talk about that too. Furthermore if there's some production elements you'd still like to add, we can look into that. More about that below.

Production

Got a song demo and need help taking it to the next level?

Then I'm your man.I love the challenge of helping someone realise their song's potential.

I grew up playing in bands, so writing songs and working on arrangments is something I love doing. I could talk about the different produciton elements I like to bring to a project, but it might be easier to just have a listen.
