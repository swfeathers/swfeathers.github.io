About

Sam, but some people call me Feathers.

I'm based in South West London and I produce music. Rock and pop is my thing, but I get excited by all good music no matter the genre.

My life in music started through playing guitar in bands like Me and the Mountain where I ended up recording, producing and mixing our own music.

I loved being in the studio crafting recordings so I gradually migrated over to that world. After managing a recording studio at in Battersea for a couple of years, I'm now back freelancing and have recently finished building a small project studio of my own that I'm calling 'The Den'. click for pics

If you're a band, artist, writer or producer and are interested in working with me then get in touch, I'd love to hear from you.
